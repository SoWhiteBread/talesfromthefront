The Devastation of Beladon's Rest

A Necromunda Underhive narrative campaign

The Setting

A long forgotten underhive dome built to surround a colossal tomb complex, Beladon's Rest is a sprawling labyrinth of rusted and decaying pre-fabricated shelters, derelict manufactories, and ancient cyclopean machinery. Recently rediscovered by enterprising Domerunners, Beladon's Rest is newly lit by muzzles flares and lasfire as the gangs of the Underhive seek to carve out new territory from the ruins.

As battle rages throughout the reclaimed hab-blocks and around industrial monoliths, rumours spread that Beladon's Rest may not be as abandoned as it seems

The Campaign

The Devastation of Beladon's Rest is based around a Dominion campaign for Necromunda Underhive with a focus on narrative and role-playing.

The players take control of the gangs and attempt to gain as much territory, fame, and glory as they can, while the Arbitrator acts as a Gamesmaster, taking control of the campaign antagonists, keeping the narrative flowing, and ensuring a relatively balanced experience.
